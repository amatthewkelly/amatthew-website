Can someone's heart be filled both with the holy spirit and satan at the same time? 

		DO YOU REALIZE HOW PRECIOUS IS YOUR BRIEF LIFE!???

My today has been one of pure existential experience. Confronted with the absurdity of consciousness and the vast emptiness of the entirety of yet-to-come time eternal, nothing seems to be more important than engaging in the experience of reality with those I love and making known to them through words affirmative and actions declarative just how very much I do so.
